What We Do

Education

We train students to think and act like data scientists - we want more people to understand data analysis and machine learning at a deeper level.

In addition, we hold public tech talks with professors and industry experts.

Experience

We offer a variety of projects that operate as subteams. We practice what we teach - real techniques with real data.

Exposure

Connect with our members during our networking events, visits, and mentoring sessions. Learn about what a career in data science is about.
